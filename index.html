The Story So Far...

The roller project has gone thru loops, ups and downs, interruptions and what not; but ultimately the irony (coincidence, serendipity, whatever you want to call it) here is I was in severe back pain for months. It turned into almost two years and simultaneously I was developing a product I came up with while in pain prior to my most severe episode.

As I pushed on with this project, through Covid, I was also pushing on with my condition. As I was invited to be on New York by Design, I was also worried I could not do the exercise segment due to pain I was still dealing with even to that point. That was more than a year ago! Crazy.

Ultimately this is the story so far; its all 100% real. Those who lived it with me be it family, friends, doctors, therapist, Pensa, pilates instructors, etc. can attest to it. Like I said, its repetitive, its self-indulging and it certainly is NOT the worst tragedy out there in the world; but for me it was very hard, it changed me and pushed me beyond where I knew I could be pushed and it my truth.

Cheers,
Ed
